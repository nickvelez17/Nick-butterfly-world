A Note on Weather:

Keep in mind, our park is mostly outdoors. In the event of extended inclement weather, we will issue a rain check when appropriate. We regularly monitor the forecast but do not make decisions based on bad weather predictions alone. See the current weather in Coconut Creek, FL by clicking HERE. Please feel free to call (954) 977-4434 during business hours if you are unsure about how the current weather situation is affecting our operation.

Specific Weather Patterns to Keep in Mind:

  • Heavy Rain/Thunderstorms: Not much flying, with lightning butterfly aviaries close temporarily until the weather improves. High possibility of early closure especially in the afternoon.
  • Light Drizzle/Cloudy: Most larger butterflies will still fly, smaller butterflies may be resting; less activity than normal. Low possibility of early closure.
  • Normal Windy: Activity is normal; butterflies & birds will fly!
  • Cold Weather (below 60 degrees Fahrenheit): Most butterflies will not fly; a great day for pictures, lots of perching on leaves and resting. Birds will be very active!
  • Sunny & Warm (above 60 degrees Fahrenheit): Best day for lots of butterfly activity. Butterflies are heat-loving, cold-blooded animals, the hotter the better!

Weather varies greatly from city to city and even street to street in Florida. Be sure to call us if you have any doubts regarding our hours, and butterfly activity.
